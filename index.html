About Me

Junior Software Engineer

The one consistent theme throughout my career has been perseverance. After receiving a degree from the University of Texas at Austin for International Relations and Global Studies and a minor in business, I started my own business. Following a successful few years operating in the entertainment/nightlife industry, 2020 presented an opportunity to pivot my expertise. With the furniture industry on the rise after the pandemic, I was offered a position at Mattress Firm. After less than one year with the company, I was offered a promotion. Not only was I one of the only women in management, but I was also the youngest manager on staff at the time. My first year as a manager, I exceeded all sales expectations and put my division in the black for the first time in years. Although this was an exciting moment in my career with the company, I decided I was ready for a new challenge. I quit my job after receiving the Million Dollar Writer award and attended a coding bootcamp with the University of Texas at Austin. This portfolio serves as a testiment to my coding abilities and love for software engineering. Although my background might cover a few different industries, the one consistent theme is my perseverance.
